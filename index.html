Who We Are

More than a club —
a movement.

AWS Cloud Club BUK is an official Amazon Web Services student community at Bayero University Kano. We exist to democratize cloud computing education and create a generation of cloud-confident engineers from northern Nigeria.

Through hands-on workshops, hackathons, certification drives, and industry mentorship, we're building the infrastructure of the future — one student at a time.
